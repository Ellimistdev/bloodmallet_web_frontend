Hunter Theorycrafters determined having profiles in SimulationCraft and therefore profiles being used on/by bloodmallet.com front-page causes more harm than good. Therefore they removed their profiles and don't intend to re-add them.

bloodmallet.com should provide helpful information. Therefore if spec/class theorycrafters decide information shown on the front-page here is misleading and absolutely bad, the data is rather deleted than publish.

The intention for bloodmallet.com is to be helpful. Not an annoyance.

Custom charts for Patrons (FAQ) can continue to get generated for Hunter characters. You need to provide your own character information ("/simc") to generate a chart specific to your character and your selected fight style.
