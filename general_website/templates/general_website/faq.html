What is bloodmallet.com?

bloodmallet.com offers DPS simulation data for everyone for World of Warcraft. Comparing different setups often requires simulations. To make this information more accessible bloodmallet.com offers a wide range of prepared simulation data. This way you can get your overview a lot quicker and get back to gaming faster.

Prepared information is a great starting point, but the exact context of your character is missing and that can change the outcome. Patrons of this project are able to create their own custom charts with their own character input. This way they can get information specific to their character.

If you enjoy this resource and want to create your own charts? Become a Patron:

How is the data generated?

SimulationCraft (open source) is used to generate all the data you can find here. The input generation for SimulationCraft and process management are done by bloodytools (open source).

What settings are used to run simulations?

Setting Value
Profile/Tier DF4 (Dragonflight: Season 4)
Target Error 0.1%
Iterations 60000 (sufficient to reach target_error)
Max Time 300 seconds
Vary Combat Length 20%
Fixed Time true
Default Actions true
Optimize Expressions true
Ready Trigger true

What are these fight styles?

CastingPatchwerk is a non-movement fight with 1 permanent enemy.

CastingPatchwerk3 is a non-movement fight with 3 permanent enemies.

CastingPatchwerk5 is a non-movement fight with 5 permanent enemies.

Are the charts up to date?

Each chart shows its generation UTC date timestamp in its subtitle.

Can I trust the general charts?

Yes-ish - they offer an overview. Your own equipment (e.g. trinkets) and talents make your character unique. Therefore, simulating your own character is the most effective way to know which performs best. Become a patron to be able to create your own custom charts specific to your character.

Can I use the charts/data from bloodmallet.com?

Yes! Of course! All data is free to use for everyone. Just one suggestion: Don't take screenshots from charts to show data. Screenshots tend to quickly become outdated. Instead have a look at this guide on how to import charts. With this you can very easily add any chart you want to your page directly and always get the most up to date information. A shout-out and some support would be appreciated. Or: Link directly to bloodmallet.com/chart/shaman/elemental/trinkets/castingpatchwerk and let your users explore charts here.
